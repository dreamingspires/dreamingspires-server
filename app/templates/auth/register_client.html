Register as a client

At Dreaming Spires, we seek to link first-class programming talent into your research. Through us, you can gain access to our wide talent pool, helping solve computational problems your organisation faces in areas such as data processing, porting old software, and writing bespoke code.

Our developers are selected to be of a high standard through our interview process. You can click here to see our portfolio of work.

How does it work?

Once verified as an organisation, you gain access to our online marketplace where your programming tasks can be submitted. The job notices will be made available to our developers who can register interest to work on the project.

Getting started

To become a verified organisation, you must first create a user account. Afterwards, you will be able to create a new organisation or join an existing one.

To create a new organisation, you will be required to provide proof of leadership of the organisation. This will include registering your account with an email address from your organisation, and providing supporting documentation.

To join an existing organisation, you should request that the leader of the organisation add you.
