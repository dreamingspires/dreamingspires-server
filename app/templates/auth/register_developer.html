At Dreaming Spires, we find interesting programming work from organisations across the world, and give you the opportunity to earn money, improve your skills, and develop your CV by taking it on.

Becoming a developer

To become a developer on Dreaming Spires, you must first register an account, submitting the required information for the verification process.

We will assess your application and may invite you for an online interview via email. The interview will consist of a short chat where we get a feel for your experience level, and will involve various problems designed to test your abilities. It will take no more than 20 minutes.

Provided your application is successful, you will become a registered developer! You will be able to take on jobs from the marketplace, and begin working on interesting problems for our clients.
